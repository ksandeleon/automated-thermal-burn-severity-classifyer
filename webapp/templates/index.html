Burn Severity Classifications
First Degree

Superficial burns affecting only the epidermis layer

• Redness • Mild swelling • Dry skin
Second Degree

Partial-thickness burns affecting epidermis and dermis

• Blisters • Severe pain • Swelling
Third Degree

Full-thickness burns through all skin layers

• White/charred • No sensation • Thick eschar
Fourth Degree

Deep burns destroying skin, tissue, muscle, and bone

• Blackened tissue • Bone exposure • Complete numbness
